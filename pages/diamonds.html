GRADING PHILOSOPHY

STRICT!

While diamond grading standards have loosened in recent years, even at renowned labs, we adhere to old-school strictness. If a diamond is between two grades, we assign the lower grade. We're never lenient, because a single grade change can result in a 25% price difference.

We reject hundreds of diamonds with inaccurate certificates that are vastly over-graded. Our industry reputation is built on being extremely discerning graders.

When you view diamonds at our store, you're seeing only accurately graded, conservatively assessed diamonds of the finest cuts. This allows you to compare confidently and select the one that's perfect for you.

THE FOUR C's of DIAMONDS

CUT

The cut determines how well a diamond interacts with light, affecting its brilliance and sparkle. We select diamonds with exceptional cuts that maximize their beauty

CLARITY

Clarity measures the presence of inclusions and blemishes. Our diamonds range from flawless to those with minor inclusions, sometimes even adding to the unique beauty of each stone

COLOR

The color grade assesses the absence of color in a diamond. We offer a range from colorless to near-colorless diamonds that emit exceptional brilliance

CARAT

Carat refers to a diamond's weight, which influences its size. We provide various carat options to suit different preferences and budgets
